The Wolf on Wall Street

2014/04/01 - Kristoffer Nesvik

(ObsidianPilot.com): Meet Jordan Belfort. Self proclaimed «Wolf of Wall Street», High-rolling millionaire at 26, snorting coke, popping pills and slamming prostitutes on a daily basis. According to himself, that's' how you make it big on Wall Street. The way Martin Scorese portray the cynic millionaire is delightfully old-school (Remember GoodFellas), and makes poor Gordon Gekko look like a pup in comparison.

The movie is based on the true story about the real Jordan Belfort (Portrayed by Leonardo DiCaprio), from his humble beginnings at L.F Rotschild; job description «smile and dial», to the rise and fall of his multi-million venture Stratton Oakmont.

Following the 1987 stock market crash leading to the fall of L.F Rotschild, Belfort joins a penny-stocks firm in Long Island, and with the help from a bunch of expert salesmen» (Mainly weed), including Mark Hanna (Matthew McConaughey) and Donnie Azoff (Jonah Hill), he sets out to form Stratton Oakmont.
