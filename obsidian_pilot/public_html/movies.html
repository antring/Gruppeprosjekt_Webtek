The Wolf of Wall Street

Meet Jordan Belfort. Self proclaimed «Wolf of Wall Street», High-rolling millionaire at 26, snorting coke, popping pills and slamming prostitutes on a daily basis. According to himself, that's' how you make it big on Wall Street. The way Martin Scorese portray the cynic millionaire is delightfully old-school (Remember GoodFellas), and makes poor Gordon Gekko look like a pup in comparison.


Only God Forgives

Only God Forgives is the latest film by Danish director/auteur Nicolas Winding Refn and it stars Ryan Gosling as the main character. Because of these two factors one could be quick to assume that it would be similar to the director's last film, Drive, also starring Ryan Gosling. The similarities are there for sure, but this film is its own beast, not relying on Drive's success with the general public. The film is significantly more artistic and ethereal, relying heavily on symbolism and hidden themes instead of a straightforward plot.

Rush

Rush is based on the true story that took place in the 1970s in formula 1 racing, were we follow the real-life rivalry between formula one racing legends James Hunt and Nikki Lauda.
